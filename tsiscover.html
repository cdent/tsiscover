tsiscover

Hi

tsiscover is an experimental tool to help you find interesting spaces to include in yours. Spaces can provide changes to the behavior, look, language and content of your space.

Each blue block comes from pre-defined tags. Space makers would be made aware of the relevant tags. Note that some of these spaces have the incorrect tags as a result of included SiteInfo tiddlers. Newer spaces are always created with their own SiteInfo so this problem will eventually fade away.

Click on close buttons to remove content you aren't interested in. Reload the page to get it all back. Click on a space name to see its SiteInfo, and once implemented, choose to include the space.

The actual include functionality does not yet work. The other main issue is that links in the SiteInfo link into the current space, not their home. Not sure if this can be resolved. Colors and styling are just bootstrap defaults. Something more awesome could come with feedback.
